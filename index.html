About Us

Haramaya University Agriculture and Environmental Students Association (HUAESA) is a student-led organization dedicated to fostering academic excellence, practical experience, and community engagement among students of the College of Agriculture and Environmental Sciences.

Our Vision

To be the leading student association in Ethiopia fostering environmentally conscious and innovative agricultural leaders.

Our Mission

To empower students with practical skills, professional development, and sustainable agricultural knowledge through hands-on training, research, and community involvement.

Programs & Projects

Academic Workshops

Research and skills enhancement through seminars and field training.

Environmental Campaigns

Tree planting, clean-up drives, and awareness for sustainability.

Entrepreneurship

Promoting agribusiness and innovation among students.
